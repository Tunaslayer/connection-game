ⓘ How to Play

Goal

Place pieces to build a continuous path connecting your two edges. First player to complete the connection wins.

Pieces

Each player holds all 19 pieces (5 tetrominoes and 14 pentominoes). The two 2×2 square pieces have no tag ends and cannot be captured. Click a piece in your panel to select it, then click the board to place it. Press R to rotate and F to flip before placing. Captured pieces return to your hand, so you can place them again.

Capture Rule

Every connected group has tag ends — one cell beyond each degree-1 tip of the group. If you place pieces that cover all in-bounds tag ends of an opponent group, that entire group is captured and its pieces go into your hand.

A straight chain has two tag ends (one beyond each tip). An L-shape or branching group also has exactly two tag ends — one per endpoint. Groups touching a board edge may have fewer in-bounds tag ends, making them harder to capture.
